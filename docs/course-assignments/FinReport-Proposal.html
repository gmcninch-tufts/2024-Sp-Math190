Proposal

due [2024-03-29 Friday] on gradescope.

The aim of the proposal is to articulate a clearly defined goal for your analysis, and to briefly discuss how it you will carry out the investigation.

You should keep the proposal to a single page (and please use standard paper- and font- sizes), with a second page for references if necessary.

  • Full statement of the problem to be investigated. This includes any constraints you may have to consider.

  • Clear statement of the goal of your investigation.

  • Concise description of what computation(s) you will need to do for the project.

  • Brief assessment of the interest of the problem (to you, or to others).

  • Cite a few references for the ideas and/or data of your investigation. (This can just be a book.)
